My projects

Welcome to My Projects page, a space where I showcase a selection of my work. Here, you can get a glimpse of various projects I've undertaken

In one of my projects, I used an Arduino and a temperature sensor designed for detecting when tea reaches the perfect drinking temperature. The setup includes a temperature sensor that continually monitors the water temperature. If it falls below the desired 52°C (125.6°F), an LED indicator starts blinking. This straightforward system ensures that when you drink your tea, it's at the ideal temperature.
